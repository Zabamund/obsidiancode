obsidiancode.io is the Open-source platform I have created to showcase my development into the coding world.
It is all about learning and practising how to code, sharing with, learning from and teaching to others.
I focus on Javascript, TypeScript, Angular, Python and SQL but am happy to stray onto to other technologies as and when I need them.
My forays into the coding world include web design and development, data science and analysis (building on years of experience as a geologist) and any aspect of digitising tasks in the modern world.
